Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • 1/8 cup vegetable oil
  • 8 oz can tomato sauce
  • 6 stems cilantro (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 4 cups water (or 4 cups low sodium chicken broth - and no bouillon)
  • 1 cube chicken flavored bouillon
  • dash of cumin
  • dash of garlic pepper

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan on medium heat.
  2. Add rice and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add 1 chicken flavored bouilloncube to 4 cups of water and microwave for 3 minutes.
  4. When rice is brown, add water, tomato sauce, salt, garlic, cumin, and garlic papper to pan.
  5. Stir and cover pan. Let simmer for 30-40 minutes or until all is cooked and there is no liquid left. Fluff before serving and ENJOY!
